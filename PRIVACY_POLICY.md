# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** November 9, 2025
**Effective Date:** November 9, 2025

FileOut ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our document generation platform at https://fileout.online.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Personal Information
- **Account Information:** Email address, name, password (encrypted)
- **Profile Data:** Username, display preferences
- **Billing Information:** Payment method details (processed securely through third-party payment processors)

### 1.2 Document Data
- **Content:** Prompts, generated documents, uploaded files
- **Metadata:** Document type, creation date, file size, generation settings
- **Usage Data:** Credits consumed, generation history

### 1.3 Technical Information
- **Device Information:** IP address, browser type, operating system
- **Usage Analytics:** Pages visited, features used, session duration
- **Cookies:** Authentication tokens, preference settings

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:
- **Provide Services:** Generate documents based on your prompts
- **Manage Accounts:** Authentication, subscription management, credit tracking
- **Improve Platform:** Analyze usage patterns, optimize AI models, enhance features
- **Customer Support:** Respond to inquiries, troubleshoot issues
- **Security:** Detect fraud, prevent abuse, ensure platform integrity
- **Communications:** Send service updates, billing notifications (transactional emails only)

## 3. Data Storage and Security

### 3.1 Storage
- **Documents:** Stored securely on Supabase cloud storage with encryption at rest
- **Database:** PostgreSQL with encrypted connections and regular backups
- **Retention:** Documents retained for 90 days after account deletion unless requested otherwise

### 3.2 Security Measures
- Industry-standard encryption (TLS 1.3 for data in transit)
- Password hashing with bcrypt
- Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
- Access controls and authentication protocols
- Automated backup systems

## 4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We **do not sell** your personal information. We may share data only in these circumstances:

### 4.1 Service Providers
- **AI Model Providers:** OpenRouter (for document generation)
- **Payment Processors:** Stripe/PayPal (for billing)
- **Cloud Infrastructure:** Supabase, AWS (for hosting and storage)

### 4.2 Legal Requirements
- Compliance with laws, regulations, or legal processes
- Protection of our rights, property, or safety
- Prevention of fraud or security threats

### 4.3 Business Transfers
- In the event of merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (users will be notified)

## 5. Your Rights and Choices

### 5.1 Access and Control
- **View Data:** Access your account information and documents via dashboard
- **Update Information:** Edit profile and preferences at any time
- **Download Data:** Export all your documents and account data
- **Delete Account:** Request complete account deletion (irreversible)

### 5.2 Communication Preferences
- **Opt-Out:** Unsubscribe from marketing emails (transactional emails still sent)
- **Notifications:** Manage email notification settings in dashboard

### 5.3 Cookies
- **Browser Settings:** Disable cookies (may affect functionality)
- **Essential Cookies:** Required for authentication and security

## 6. Data Protection Rights (GDPR/CCPA)

If you are located in the EU, EEA, UK, or California, you have additional rights:

### 6.1 GDPR Rights (EU/EEA/UK)
- **Right to Access:** Request copies of your personal data
- **Right to Rectification:** Correct inaccurate information
- **Right to Erasure:** Request deletion of your data
- **Right to Restrict Processing:** Limit how we use your data
- **Right to Data Portability:** Receive data in machine-readable format
- **Right to Object:** Oppose processing of your data
- **Right to Withdraw Consent:** Revoke consent at any time

### 6.2 CCPA Rights (California)
- **Right to Know:** What data we collect and how it's used
- **Right to Delete:** Request deletion of personal information
- **Right to Opt-Out:** Prevent sale of data (we don't sell data)
- **Right to Non-Discrimination:** Equal service regardless of privacy choices
